# Bridge Question List v1

Open questions requiring Stage II data to answer.

## Q1: Talk test validity
Does the heart rate at which a patient cannot speak full sentences (talk test HR)
correlate with VT1_HR (r > 0.70) in older adults with HTN?

## Q2: 6MWT as VO2peak proxy
Does 6MWT distance / predicted 6MWT (Enright equation) predict VO2peak%predicted
with sufficient precision for zone classification (kappa ≥ 0.60)?

## Q3: EIH detection at home
Can consumer-grade fingertip pulse oximetry during 6MWT reliably detect EIH
(sensitivity ≥ 80%, specificity ≥ 85%)?

## Q4: HRV as O2 pulse surrogate
Does resting 5-min HRV RMSSD correlate with O2 pulse peak (r > 0.50)?

## Q5: RPE calibration
At what RPE level do patients in yellow zone typically reach their RCP HR?
Is RPE 14 a reliable substitute for T2 anchor?
